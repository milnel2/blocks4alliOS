Switch Control User Study

We want to improve how Blocks4All works with Switch Control! We are hoping to recruit children/young adults aged 5-21 with motor impairments who use a switch or switch control to interact with a computer. We are interested in having them test out Blocks4All to program a robot using switch control on an iPad.

We are interested in recruiting both local participants (Minnesota and Washington) and remote participants who would take part in the study over video conference. Each participant would complete tasks to program a robot in 3-4 sessions that are 45-60 minutes long. They would receive a $30 Amazon.com gift card for each session they take part in.

Interested? Questions? Contact the Principal Investigators, Lauren Milne at lmilne@macalester.edu or Richard Ladner at ladner@cs.washington.edu
