What is Blocks4All?

An accessible iPad app to teach computer coding with the Dash robot, designed by researchers at University of Washington and Macalester College.

It is accessible with:

  • VoiceOver,
  • Switch Control,
  • and Voice Control

The app requires the Dash robot in order to run, but is not affiliated with Wonder Workshop.
